# What is a Dividend?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

A portion of a company's profits paid directly to shareholders — usually quarterly. If you own 100 shares of a company paying a $2 annual dividend, you receive $200 per year just for owning the shares.
