# What is Rebalancing?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

Periodically restoring your portfolio to its target asset allocation. If stocks rise and bonds fall, your 80/20 portfolio becomes 88/12. Rebalancing means selling some stocks and buying bonds to return to 80/20 — forcing you to sell high and buy low automatically.
