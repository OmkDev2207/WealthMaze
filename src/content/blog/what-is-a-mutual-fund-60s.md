# What is a Mutual Fund?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

A pooled investment vehicle where thousands of investors contribute money that a professional fund manager invests collectively. You buy units of the fund rather than individual stocks — giving you instant diversification even with small amounts.
