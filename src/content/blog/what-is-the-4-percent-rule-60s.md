# What is the 4% Rule?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

A retirement withdrawal guideline suggesting you can withdraw 4% of your portfolio annually without running out of money over a 30-year retirement. A $1,000,000 portfolio supports $40,000 per year in withdrawals. Based on historical US market returns since 1926.
