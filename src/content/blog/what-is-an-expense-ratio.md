# What is an Expense Ratio?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

The annual fee a mutual fund charges you — silently deducted from returns. A 1% expense ratio on a $100,000 portfolio costs you $1,000 every year whether the fund goes up or down.
