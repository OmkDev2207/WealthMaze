# What is CAGR?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

Compound Annual Growth Rate — the smoothed annual return of an investment over a period. If $10,000 became $18,000 over 5 years, the CAGR is 12.5%. It tells you the equivalent steady annual return that would produce the same result.
