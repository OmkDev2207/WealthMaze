# What is Dollar Cost Averaging?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

Investing a fixed amount at regular intervals regardless of market conditions. When prices fall you buy more units. When prices rise you buy fewer. Over time this lowers your average cost per unit automatically.
