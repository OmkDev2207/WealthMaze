# What is Rupee Cost Averaging?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

The Indian equivalent of Dollar Cost Averaging — investing a fixed rupee amount regularly regardless of market conditions. The mechanism is identical — systematic investment at regular intervals regardless of price. Your SIP does this automatically every month.
