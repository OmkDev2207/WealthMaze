# What is a Recession?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

Two consecutive quarters of negative GDP growth. Recessions cause unemployment to rise and corporate profits to fall — which usually pushes stock prices lower. Since 1945 the US has experienced 12 recessions. Average duration: 11 months.
