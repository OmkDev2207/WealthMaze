# What is Net Worth?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

Everything you own minus everything you owe. Assets minus liabilities. A person earning $200,000 a year with $50,000 in savings and $180,000 in debt has a lower net worth than someone earning $60,000 with $150,000 saved and zero debt.
