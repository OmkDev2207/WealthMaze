# What is a Bear Market?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

A market decline of 20% or more from recent highs. Bear markets are normal — the S&P 500 has experienced 26 bear markets since 1928. Average duration: 9.6 months. Average decline: 36%. Every single one has ended.
