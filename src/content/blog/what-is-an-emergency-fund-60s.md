# What is an Emergency Fund?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

Three to six months of living expenses held in a liquid, accessible account. Not an investment — a buffer. Its purpose is to prevent a financial shock from forcing you to sell investments at the wrong time or accumulate high-interest debt.
