# What is a Bull Market?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

A sustained rise of 20% or more in market prices. The average bull market lasts 2.7 years and produces gains of approximately 114%. Bull markets last roughly three times longer than bear markets historically.
