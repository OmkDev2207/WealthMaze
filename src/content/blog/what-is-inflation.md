# What is Inflation?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

The rate at which prices rise over time — reducing purchasing power. At 5% annual inflation, $100 today buys only $61 worth of goods in 10 years. Your money doesn't disappear. It just quietly buys less every year.
