# What is Asset Allocation?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

How you divide your portfolio across different asset classes — stocks, bonds, gold, cash. The mix determines both your risk level and your expected return. A 90/10 stocks-to-bonds portfolio behaves very differently from a 60/40 split.
