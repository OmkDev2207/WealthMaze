# What is a P/E Ratio?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

Price-to-Earnings ratio — a stock's current price divided by its annual earnings per share. A P/E of 20 means you're paying $20 for every $1 of annual earnings. Lower P/E can signal undervaluation. Higher P/E suggests growth expectations are priced in.
