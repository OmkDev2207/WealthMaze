# What is a Bond?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

A loan you make to a government or company. They pay you fixed interest for a set period then return your principal at maturity. Lower risk than stocks. Lower return than stocks. Used to add stability to a portfolio.
