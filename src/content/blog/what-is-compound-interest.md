# What is Compound Interest?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

Earning interest on your interest. $1,000 at 10% becomes $1,100 after year one. Year two earns 10% on $1,100 — not $1,000. The difference seems small early. Over 30 years it turns $1,000 into $17,449.
