# What is an Index Fund?

*By Om K. | WealthMaze | Finance in 60 Seconds | 1 min read*

---

A fund that tracks a market index — like the S&P 500 — by holding all the stocks in that index in proportion to their size. No fund manager picking stocks. Lower fees. Historically outperforms 85% of actively managed funds over 15 years.
